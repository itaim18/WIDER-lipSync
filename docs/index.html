Key Findings

✅

latentsync_smooth wins

Best identity preservation (0.96), best mouth quality (LPIPS 0.106), and the cookie plate stays visible without any fix. 40 denoising steps with no DeepCache is the sweet spot.

⚠️

Occlusion fix backfires

The per-frame BiSeNet compositing is mathematically correct, but the face-parsing mask jitters between frames, causing flickering worse than the original occlusion. Needs temporally-stable segmentation (SAM2) to work.

🔄

MuseTalk: smooth but unfaithful

30% less temporal jitter than LatentSync, but identity drops from 0.96 to 0.79 and mouth distortion nearly doubles. Not ideal when the person needs to look like themselves.
